# 🛒 Walmart Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive data analysis dashboard built using Excel, based on historical sales data from **Walmart** over a period of three years (2010–2012).

The goal of this project is to analyze sales performance, identify key trends, and evaluate the impact of external factors such as temperature, fuel prices, and holidays on weekly sales.


![image alt](https://github.com/hassandata3/Walmart-Sales-Analysis-Excel-/blob/main/dashboards-walmart.png?raw=true)


## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance and trends
- Identify top-performing stores
- Evaluate seasonality and holiday impact
- Study the effect of external factors:
  - Temperature 🌡️
  - Fuel Prices ⛽
  - CPI (Inflation) 📊
- Build an interactive dashboard for better decision-making

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- 📈 **KPIs Overview**
  - Total Sales
  - Average Weekly Sales
  - Average Fuel Price
  - Holiday Impact %

- 🏆 **Top 10 Stores Analysis**
  - Comparison of highest-performing stores

- 📅 **Sales Trends**
  - Monthly sales across 3 years
  - Identification of peak periods

- 🌡️ **Temperature Impact**
  - Sales variation across temperature ranges

- ⛽ **Fuel Price Impact**
  - Analysis of fuel price influence on sales

- 🎄 **Holiday Impact**
  - Comparison between holiday and non-holiday sales

- 🎛️ **Interactive Filters**
  - Year selection (2010, 2011, 2012)
  - Season filter (Summer / Winter)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📊 **Stable Overall Performance**  
  Total sales exceeded **$6.2B**, with consistent weekly averages, indicating strong and stable demand.

- 🎄 **Seasonality is the Primary Driver**  
  Sales peak during the holiday season (Nov–Dec), making it the most critical period for business performance.

- 📈 **Holidays Increase Sales (~7%)**  
  A clear uplift in sales during holiday weeks compared to regular periods.

- 🏪 **Store Performance Variability**  
  Significant differences between top and low-performing stores highlight optimization opportunities.

- 🌡️ **Weather Influences Customer Behavior**  
  Moderate temperatures are associated with higher sales, while extreme conditions reduce activity.

- ⛽ **Limited Impact of Fuel Prices**  
  Fuel price fluctuations show only a minor effect on sales.

- 📊 **Weak Correlation with CPI**  
  Inflation does not significantly impact sales, likely due to Walmart’s pricing strategy and essential product mix.

---

## 🧠 Business Insight

> Sales are primarily driven by seasonality (holidays), while economic factors such as fuel prices and inflation have a limited impact. This reflects Walmart’s strong positioning as a low-cost retailer offering essential goods with relatively inelastic demand.
